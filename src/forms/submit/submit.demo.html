Submit

A submit buttons for forms, providing feedback to the customer.

Configuration
<form ng-submit="$ctrl.promise = $http.post(...)">
  <div class="form-group">...</div>
  <tw-submit
    label="{{ $ctrl.label }}"
    promise="$ctrl.promise"
    on-submit="$ctrl.log('submit')"
    on-success="$ctrl.log('success')"
    on-failure="$ctrl.log('failure')"
    ng-disabled="{{ $ctrl.disabled }}">
  </tw-submit>
</form>
