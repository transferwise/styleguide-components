Tw Date Lookup

The date lookup is useful for selecting dates that are relatively recent, and can be used in a pair for date ranges.

If your date is likely to be further in the past (e.g. Date of birth), use twDate instead.

Configuration
<tw-date-lookup
  placeholder="{{$ctrl.dateLookup.placeholder}}"
  locale="{{$ctrl.dateLookup.locale}}"
  ng-model={{$ctrl.model}}
  ng-required="{{$ctrl.dateLookup.required}}"
  ng-disabled="{{$ctrl.dateLookup.disabled}}"
  ng-min={{$ctrl.dateLookup.min}}
  ng-max={{$ctrl.dateLookup.max}}
  short-date="{{$ctrl.dateLookup.shortDate}}"
  ng-change="..."
  ng-focus="..."
  ng-blur="..."
  size={{$ctrl.dateLookup.size}}>
</tw-date-lookup>
