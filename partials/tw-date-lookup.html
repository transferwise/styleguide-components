Tw Date Lookup

The date lookup is useful for selecting dates that are relatively recent, and can be used in a pair for date ranges.

If your date is likely to be further in the past (e.g. Date of birth), use twDate instead.

Configuration
<tw-date-lookup
  placeholder="{{vm.dateLookup.placeholder}}"
  locale="{{vm.dateLookup.locale}}"
  ng-model={{vm.model.components.dateLookup}}
  ng-required="{{vm.dateLookup.required}}"
  ng-disabled="{{vm.dateLookup.disabled}}"
  ng-min={{vm.dateLookup.min}}
  ng-max={{vm.dateLookup.max}}
  short-date="{{vm.dateLookup.shortDate}}"
  ng-change="..."
  ng-focus="..."
  ng-blur="..."
  size={{vm.dateLookup.size}}>
</tw-date-lookup>
