Tw Date Lookup

The date lookup is useful for selecting dates that are relatively recent, and can be used in a pair for date ranges.

If your date is likely to be further in the past (e.g. Date of birth), use twDate instead.

Configuration
<tw-date-lookup
  placeholder="{{vm.dateRange.placeholder}}"
  locale="{{vm.dateRange.locale}}"
  ng-model={{vm.model.components.dateRange}}
  ng-disabled="{{vm.dateRange.disabled}}"
  ng-min={{vm.dateRange.min}}
  ng-max={{vm.dateRange.max}}
  size={{vm.dateRange.size}}>
</tw-date-lookup>
